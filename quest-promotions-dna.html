The Concept

What is Masterclass of the Month and what problem does it solve?

The Problem

Members have access to 100 quests but don't know where to start. Non-members have never experienced the quality of our content. Both need a reason to engage now.

The Solution

Masterclass of the Month is a free monthly live class series. Every month, we spotlight one quest and host a live masterclass on that topic. Each class is promoted individually â€” simple, focused, no complexity.

Two Goals, Two Audiences

Non-Members Members
Goal Convert to membership or quest purchase Get them to start the featured quest
Value for them Free taste of content quality Guided journey through quest library
Post-class action Buy membership or single quest Start the quest in app

Positioning

"Every month. One quest. One live masterclass."

Subscribe once. Get notified each month. Join the classes that interest you. It's free, it's live, and it's your guided path through 100 transformational quests.

Social Strategy

How to promote masterclasses on social media, aligned with Mindvalley's brand.

Decision

Branded template with series identity

Every Masterclass of the Month post uses a consistent template that's instantly recognizable. Clean, minimal, on-brand. Create a Canva/Figma template library â€” each month just swap the quest name and date.

Visual Style Guidelines

Aligned with Mindvalley's brand aesthetic:

  • Clean and minimal: Lots of whitespace, not cluttered
  • Color palette: Dark backgrounds (charcoal/navy) or clean white, with gold accent for premium feel
  • Typography: Bold, clear headlines. Easy to read at a glance.
  • Series badge: "MASTERCLASS OF THE MONTH" in small caps, letter-spaced
  • Human element: Feature the instructor or real imagery â€” no AI-generated visuals
  • Consistent layout: Same template structure every month for recognition

Landing Page Structure

One evergreen URL that's updated monthly: mindvalley.com/membership/masterclass

Page Sections

  1. Hero
    Series branding + this month's class. Headline, date/time, registration form
  2. Stats Bar
    Instructor credibility: hours on stage, NPS rating, students trained, etc.
  3. What You'll Discover
    3 key learnings/breakthroughs from the masterclass
  4. Meet Your Host
    Instructor bio, credentials, photo collage
  5. FAQ
    Is it free? Yes. Will there be a replay? Do I need experience? etc.
  6. Final CTA
    Last push to register with button back to form

Thank You Page (After Registration)

  • Confirmation: "You're registered! Check your email for the Zoom link."
  • Add to calendar: Google, Apple, Outlook buttons
  • Share: Social share buttons
  • App download: "Explore free content while you wait."
