3 Launch Timeline (4 Days)

A compact, high-impact launch sequence that creates urgency without overwhelming your team.

DAY 1 - ANNOUNCEMENT

Launch & Generate Buzz

  • Send announcement email to general list ‚Üí Landing page
  • Send exclusive invite email to members ‚Üí Direct RSVP link
  • Post announcement on social media (all platforms)
  • Activate in-app banner: "Master Class of the Month"
  • Schedule Live Event banner in app
  • Launch ads (if using paid promotion)
DAY 2 - VALUE STACK

Build Anticipation

  • Send "what you'll learn" email to general list
  • Send "why this quest matters" email to members
  • Post testimonial/social proof content on social
  • Send push notification to app users who haven't RSVP'd
DAY 3 - REMINDER

Final Push (Day Before)

  • Send "tomorrow's the day" email to registrants
  • Post countdown content on social media
  • Send push notification: "Class tomorrow!"
  • Optional: Behind-the-scenes story/reel
DAY 4 - LIVE CLASS

Deliver & Convert

  • Send "We're live in 2 hours" email with Zoom link
  • Send "Starting now!" push notification
  • Host live Zoom class
  • Post "Live now!" stories during class
  • Send post-class email with replay + offer (non-members)
  • Send post-class email with quest deep link (members)
